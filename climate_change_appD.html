What Is Climate Change?

Climate change is a long-term change in the Earth's weather, mainly caused by people burning fuels like coal, oil, and gas. This releases gases that trap heat in the air, making the planet warmer. It can lead to things like rising sea levels, stronger storms, and harm to animals and plants.



How can you help with climate change?

You can help with climate change by taking care of our planet! You can save energy, reduce waste, and use cleaner energy like solar power. You can also plant trees, recycle, and use less plastic. Plus, you can learn why it's important to protect the Earth and how small changes in what you do every day make a big difference for the future!
